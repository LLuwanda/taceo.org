WELCOME TO TACEO

Tanzania Community Empowerment Organization

Our Vision

"To create a future where everyone has opportunities, resources, and support to thrive, contributing positively to their communities and society at large."

Our Mission

"To empower individuals through education, advocacy, and access to resources, fostering sustainable development and collective progress within communities."

TACEO was established to address the significant need for community empowerment in Tanzania. Poverty, climate change impacts, unemployment, increasing disease rates, discrimination, and the growing number of street children are all direct results of poor living conditions facing these communities.

Our organization strives to create a safe space for youth, elders, children, and marginalized groups who are the primary targets of TACEO's efforts to identify and address the root causes of these problems.
